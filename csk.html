CHENNAI SUPER KINGS (CSK)

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
Founded in 2008.The team served a two-year suspension from the IPL starting July 2015
for the alleged involvement of their owners in the 2013 IPL betting case

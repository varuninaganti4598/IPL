ROYAL CHALLENGERS BANGALORE (RCB)

The Royal Challengers Bangalore (often abbreviated as RCB) are a franchise cricket team based in Bangalore, Karnataka, that plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
It was founded in 2008 by United Spirits and named after the company's liquor brand Royal Challenge.
Since its inception, the team has played its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.

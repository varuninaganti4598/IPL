MUMBAI INDIANS (MI)

The Mumbai Indians is a franchise cricket team based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, that competes in the Indian Premier League. Founded in 2008, the team is owned by India's biggest conglomerate, Reliance Industries.
Since its establishment, the team has played its home matches in the 33,108-capacity Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai.
They are 4 times IPL winner and also they are defending champions.

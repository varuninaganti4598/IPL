KOLKATA KNIGHT RIDERS (KKR)

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Kolkata in the Indian Premier League.
The franchise is owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan, actress Juhi Chawla and her spouse Jay Mehta.
The home of the Knight Riders is the iconic Eden Gardens stadium.

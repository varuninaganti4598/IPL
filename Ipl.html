INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE 2020

ABOUT IPL :

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India.
Contested during March or April and May of every year by eight teams representing eight different cities or states in India.
The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2008.
The IPL is 20-20 format Cricket.
